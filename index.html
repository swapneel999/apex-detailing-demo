OUR METHOD

THE PRECISION
PROCESS

01

PAINT ASSESSMENT

Every vehicle begins with a comprehensive paint thickness gauge reading and swirl assessment under controlled LED lighting. We document baseline condition before any work begins.

02

DECONTAMINATION

Chemical decontamination with a pH-neutral wash, followed by iron fallout remover and a full clay bar treatment to achieve a microscopically clean surface before machine work.

03

CORRECTION & COATING

Machine polishing removes oxidation, swirl marks, and light scratches. The vehicle is IPA wiped and coated in a controlled environment to maximise ceramic bond strength and longevity.
