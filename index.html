Our Services

Ceramic Coating

Advanced ceramic coatings form a durable, transparent layer that shields your vehicle from UV rays, environmental contaminants, and harsh elements — while deepening gloss and shine.

Paint Correction

State-of-the-art machine polishing removes swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation — restoring your vehicle's paint to its original glory with single or multi-stage correction.

Premium Detailing

Meticulous detailing packages covering every inch inside and out — steam extraction, leather conditioning, compressed-air vacuuming, and UV dressing for a showroom finish.

Pre-Sale Detail

Maximise your vehicle's resale value with a full decontamination, paint enhancement, engine bay clean, and deep interior restoration. Present your car at its absolute best.

Our Method

The Precision
Process

01

Paint Assessment

Every vehicle begins with a comprehensive paint thickness gauge reading and swirl assessment under controlled LED lighting. Baseline condition is fully documented before any work begins.

02

Decontamination

Chemical decontamination with a pH-neutral wash, followed by iron fallout remover and a full clay bar treatment to achieve a microscopically clean surface before machine work begins.

03

Correction & Coating

Machine polishing removes oxidation, swirl marks, and light scratches. The vehicle is IPA-wiped and coated in a controlled environment to maximise ceramic bond strength and longevity.
