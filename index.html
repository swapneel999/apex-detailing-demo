Our Heritage

Crafted with uncompromising authenticity.

We don't do shortcuts. Every cup of chai is brewed using whole, freshly ground spices—cinnamon, cardamom, and clove—simmered slowly to extract maximum flavor.

Our food menu is a direct homage to the bustling street carts of Mumbai, elevated with premium local Geelong ingredients and presented with the care of a modern editorial kitchen. It's the tactile energy of India, poured into a cup.
